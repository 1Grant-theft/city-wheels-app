Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit or debit card in your name, and to be at least 21 years of age.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic liability insurance is included. We also offer premium collision damage waivers at the counter for extra peace of mind.

Can I pick up the car at one location and return it to another?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals! Please contact our support team to arrange the specific drop-off location as additional fees may apply.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation for free up to 48 hours before your scheduled pick-up time. Late cancellations may incur a 1-day rental fee.
